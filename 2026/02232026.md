## 二次多項式進階題

楊著《代數是什麼？》〈無理方根的簡易計算〉p.103 習題4：

(1) 若 $x = a + \sqrt{a^2-1}\; (a>1)$，求 $x^3 + \dfrac{1}{x^3}$。

解方：
$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{1}{x} &= \frac{1}{a + \sqrt{a^2-1}} \\
&= \frac{a - \sqrt{a^2-1}}{(a+\sqrt{a^2-1})(a-\sqrt{a^2-1})} \\
&= \frac{a-\sqrt{a^2-1}}{a^2 - (\sqrt{a^2-1})^2} \\
&= a - \sqrt{a^2 - 1}
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
\implies x + \frac{1}{x} = (a + \sqrt{a^2-1}) + (a - \sqrt{a^2-1}) = 2a
\qquad\cdots\text{(1)}
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
x^3 + \frac{1}{x^3} &= (x+\frac{1}{x})^3 - 3x^2\cdot\frac{1}{x} - 3x(\frac{1}{x})^2 \\
&= (x+\frac{1}{x})^3 - 3x - 3\cdot\frac{1}{x} \\
&= (x+\frac{1}{x})^3 - 3(x+\frac{1}{x}) \\
\text{（用(1)式代入）}&= (2a)^3 - 3(2a) \\
&= 8a^3 - 6a
\end{aligned}$$

(2) 若 $x=\dfrac{1}{3}$，求
$\dfrac{2(1+2\sqrt{x})}{1-\sqrt{x}} - \dfrac{1-\sqrt{x}}{1+2\sqrt{x}}$。

解方：
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{1+2\sqrt{x}}{1-\sqrt{x}} &=
\dfrac{(1+2\sqrt{x})(1+\sqrt{x})}{(1-\sqrt{x})(1+\sqrt{x})} \\
&= \dfrac{1+2x+3\sqrt{x}}{1-x} \\
x\text{ 用 }\frac{1}{3}\text{ 代入：} \\
&= \dfrac{1+\frac{2}{3}+3\sqrt{\frac{1}{3}}}{1-\frac{1}{3}} \\
&= \dfrac{3+2+9\sqrt{\frac{1}{3}}}{2} \\
&= \dfrac{5+3\sqrt{3}}{2}
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
抑是\ x\ 用\ \frac{1}{3} 直接代入\ \dfrac{1+2\sqrt{x}}{1-\sqrt{x}} &=
\dfrac{1+2\sqrt{\frac13}}{1-\sqrt\frac13} \\
&= \dfrac{1+2\cdot\frac{\sqrt3}{3}}{1-\frac{\sqrt3}{3}} \\
&= \dfrac{3+2\sqrt3}{3-\sqrt3} \\
&= \dfrac{(3+2\sqrt3)(3+\sqrt3)}{(3-\sqrt3)(3+\sqrt3)} \\
&= \dfrac{9+9\sqrt3+6}{6} \\
&= \dfrac{15+9\sqrt3}{6} \\
&= \dfrac{5+3\sqrt3}{2}

\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
原式 &= 2\cdot\dfrac{5+3\sqrt{3}}{2} - \dfrac{2}{5+3\sqrt{3}} \\
&= 5+3\sqrt3 - \dfrac{2(5-3\sqrt3)}{(5+3\sqrt3)(5-3\sqrt3)} \\
&= 5+3\sqrt3 - \dfrac{2(5-3\sqrt3)}{25-27} \\
&= 5 + 3\sqrt3 + 5 - 3\sqrt3 \\
&= 10
\end{aligned}$$

(3) 若 $x = \dfrac{2ab}{b^2+1}\; (a>0,\ b>0)$，求
$\dfrac{\sqrt{a+x}+\sqrt{a-x}}{\sqrt{a+x}-\sqrt{a-x}}$。

解方：

原式分母、分子同乘 $(\sqrt{a+x} + \sqrt{a-x})$：

$$\begin{aligned}
原式 &= \frac{(\sqrt{a+x}+\sqrt{a-x})^2}{a+x - (a-x)} \\
&= \frac{a+x + a-x + 2\sqrt{(a+x)(a-x)}}{2x} \\
&= \frac{2a+2\sqrt{(a+x)(a-x)}}{2x} \\
&= \frac{a+\sqrt{(a+x)(a-x)}}{x} \qquad\cdots\text{(1)}
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
a + x &= a + \frac{2ab}{b^2+1} \\
&= \frac{ab^2 + a + 2ab}{b^2+1} \\
&= \frac{a(b^2+2b+1)}{b^2+1} \\
&= \frac{a(b+1)^2}{b^2+1} \\
\text{又 } a - x &= \frac{a(b-1)^2}{b^2+1}
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
\sqrt{(a+x)(a-x)} &= \sqrt{\frac{a(b+1)^2}{b^2+1}\cdot\frac{a(b-1)^2}{b^2+1}} \\
&= \frac{a(b+1)|b-1|}{b^2+1} \\
&= \frac{a|b^2-1|}{b^2+1}
\end{aligned}$$

代入 (1) 式：

$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{a+\dfrac{a(b^2-1)}{b^2+1}}{\dfrac{2ab}{b^2+1}}
&= \frac{a(b^2+1) + a|b^2-1|}{2ab} \qquad\cdots\text{(2)}
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{cases}
b >= 1 \implies (2) = \dfrac{2ab^2}{2ab} = b \\
0 < b < 1 \implies (2) = \dfrac{2a}{2ab} = \dfrac{1}{b}
\end{cases}$$

(4) 若 $2x = a + \dfrac{1}{a},\ 2y = b + \dfrac{1}{b}\; (a>0,\ b>0)$，求
$2(x\times y - \sqrt{x^2-1}\sqrt{y^2-1})$

解方：

$$\begin{aligned}
2x &= a + \frac1a \\
x &= \frac{1}{2}(a+\frac1a) \\
x^2 &= \frac14\left(a^2+2a\cdot\frac1a+\frac{1}{a^2}\right) \\
x^2 &= \frac14(a^2 + 2 + \frac{1}{a^2}) \\
x^2 - 1 &= \frac14(a^2 - 2 + \frac{1}{a^2}) \\
x^2 - 1 &= \left(\frac12(a - \frac1a)\right)^2 \\
\sqrt{x^2-1} &= \left|\frac12(a-\frac1a)\right| \\
\text{同理}\quad \sqrt{y^2 - 1} &= \left|\frac12(b - \frac1b)\right|
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
原式 &= 2\left(\frac{a+\frac1a}{2} \times \frac{b+\frac1b}{2} -
  \left|\frac{a-\frac1a}{2} \right|\left|\frac{b-\frac1b}{2}\right| \right) \\
&= 2\times\frac14 \left((a+\frac1a)(b+\frac1b) - \left|a-\frac1a\right|\left|b-\frac1b\right| \right)
\end{aligned}$$

兩个絕對值相乘，結果可能無變號，可能有變號，兩種可能爾爾：

$$\begin{cases}
無變號：\frac12 \left( (ab+\frac{a}{b}+\frac{b}{a}+\frac{1}{ab}) -
(ab-\frac{a}{b}-\frac{b}{a}+\frac{1}{ab})  \right) = \frac{a}{b}+\frac{b}{a}\\
有變號：\frac12 \left( (ab+\frac{a}{b}+\frac{b}{a}+\frac{1}{ab}) +
(ab-\frac{a}{b}-\frac{b}{a}+\frac{1}{ab})  \right) = ab + \frac{1}{ab}
\end{cases}$$

(5) 若 $x=\frac{\sqrt3}{2}$，求 $\dfrac{1+x}{1+\sqrt{1+x}} +
\dfrac{1-x}{1-\sqrt{1-x}}$。

解方：

$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{\sqrt3}{2} 代入 \sqrt{1+x} &= \sqrt{1+\frac{\sqrt3}{2}} \\
&= \sqrt{2+\sqrt3 \over 2} \\
&= \sqrt{4+2\sqrt3 \over 4} \qquad\text{（一點訣！）} \\
&= \sqrt{(\frac{\sqrt{3}+1}{2})^2} \\
&= \frac{\sqrt3 + 1}{2} \\
\text{同理：}\sqrt{1-x} &= \frac{\sqrt3-1}{2}
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{1+x}{1+\sqrt{1+x}} + \dfrac{1-x}{1-\sqrt{1-x}} &=
  \dfrac{1+\frac{\sqrt3}{2}}{1 + \frac{\sqrt3+1}{2}} +
  \dfrac{1-\frac{\sqrt3}{2}}{1 - \frac{\sqrt3-1}{2}} \\
&= { 2+\sqrt3 \over 2+\sqrt3+1} + { 2-\sqrt3 \over 2-\sqrt3+1} \\
&= {2+\sqrt3 \over 3+\sqrt3} + { 2-\sqrt3 \over 3-\sqrt3 } \\
&= { (6-2\sqrt3+3\sqrt3-3)+(6+2\sqrt3-3\sqrt3-3) \over 9-3} \\
&= { 3+\sqrt3+3-\sqrt3 \over 6} \\
&= 1
\end{aligned}$$
